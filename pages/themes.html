We've created several custom Omeka themes specifically for use with Neatline exhibits. Follow the links below to download theme files for your project. See our Theme Tutorials for guides on installing and customizing themes for your Neatline exhibits. Neatline does not require custom theming, you're welcome to use the themes provided by Omeka for your site.

NeatLight

NeatLight is a minimal theme for Omeka that foregrounds Neatline exhibits and narratives. It was designed specifically for use with the NeatlineText plugin, to position the narrative text next to the exhibit inside of a scrollable container element.
